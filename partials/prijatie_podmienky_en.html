Admission requirements for Bachelor degree study

In order to be admitted into the faculty, the applicant has to meet the following requirements:

1

A complete secondary education or secondary vocational education.

2

Earning at least 40 points in the admission process.

3

Capacity constrains of the faculty as well as the applicants' study program preferences are taken into account during the admissions. The system of the admission process guarantees maximum possible objectivity and transparency.

Evaluation of the Admission Process

The applicant receives a number of points equal to the highest percentile attained in one of selected written exams, plus extra points for achievements in selected competitions. The results of the following exams are taken into account for applicants for the 3-year study programs,

  • Evaluation of the matriculation examination in mathemathics.
  • Evaluation of a test in mathematics conducted and verified by an independent expert.

For applicants for the 4-year study programs, the results of the following exams are taken into account:

  • Evaluation of the matriculation examination in mathemathics.
  • Evaluation of a test in mathematics conducted and verified by an independent expert.
  • Evaluation of a scholastic aptitude test conducted and verified by an independent expert.

See also the evaluation of competitions.
