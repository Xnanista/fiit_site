If the faculty identifies a high school competition in informatics or mathematics, it may take its results into account. If the results of such competition are considered, the faculty must publish the evaluation method at the latest two months before the admission date (does nto apply to CCNA certificates from Cisco Networking Academy).

The condition of being awarded points is sending a verified certificate as an attachment with the study application.

An applicant who took part in multiple competitions is awarded points only for that competition in the given category that earns them the most bonus points.
